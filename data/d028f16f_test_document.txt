CONSTRUCTION PROJECT: Diriyah Gate Development
Location: Riyadh, Saudi Arabia
Client: Diriyah Gate Development Authority

PROJECT SPECIFICATIONS:
- Total Area: 1,500,000 square meters
- Building Footprint: 450,000 square meters
- Number of Buildings: 28 heritage-style buildings
- Maximum Height: 45 meters (heritage compliance)
- Foundation Type: Deep pile foundation (25m depth)
- Structural System: Reinforced concrete with traditional Najdi facade

MATERIAL QUANTITIES (Estimated):
1. Concrete: 285,000 cubic meters
   - C40 grade for foundations
   - C35 grade for structural elements
   - C30 grade for non-structural elements

2. Reinforcement Steel: 42,500 metric tons
   - Grade 60 (420 MPa) for main reinforcement
   - Grade 40 (280 MPa) for distribution bars

3. Formwork: 890,000 square meters
   - Traditional timber formwork for heritage elements
   - Modern system formwork for standard structures

4. Masonry: 125,000 cubic meters
   - Local limestone for exterior walls
   - Traditional mud brick for heritage features

5. Roofing: 320,000 square meters
   - Traditional wood and palm frond roofing
   - Modern waterproofing membrane underneath

PROJECT TIMELINE:
- Phase 1: Foundation (18 months)
- Phase 2: Structure (24 months)
- Phase 3: Facade (18 months)
- Phase 4: MEP and Finishes (12 months)
Total Duration: 72 months

ESTIMATED COST: SAR 4.2 billion (USD 1.12 billion)
